History of MMF

The MMF's journey began in a modest community center, equipped with little more than a handful of instruments and a burning passion to make a difference. Early on, the Foundation focused on providing free music lessons to children in the local community, often reaching out to those in under served neighborhoods where music education was considered a luxury far beyond reach.
