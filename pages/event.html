Join Us for the Back-to-School Fundraiser Gala

Date: August 25, 2024

Location: Le Jardin Étoilé, Route Royale, Antananarivo, Madagascar

As summer winds down and the anticipation of a new school year begins, Music Makes Futures (MMF) invites you to an evening filled with elegance, inspiration, and purpose. Mark your calendars for August 25, 2024, as we host our annual Back-to-School Fundraiser Gala at the prestigious Le Jardin Étoilé, Route Royale, Antananarivo, Madagascar. This gala isn't just an event; it's a celebration of hope, a testament to the power of education, and a night that promises to make a significant difference in the lives of children in Madagascar.

Why Attend?

The Back-to-School Fundraiser Gala is more than a gathering; it's a pivotal moment where you can directly impact the educational journey of countless children. Every ticket purchased, every auction item bid on, and every donation made during the gala will go directly towards supporting MMF's comprehensive education programs. From school supplies and tuition fees to teacher salaries and educational materials, your support will lay the foundation for a brighter, more promising future for the children we serve.
